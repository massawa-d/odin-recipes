Surf and turf fried rice Recipe

Description

This easy fried rice recipe will have you making this every meal. It is really so simple and all can be done in one pan. I like using a wok, I got a great non-stick one at Ikea for the stove top, but you can also use a large non-stick pan as well!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Steak
  • Shrimp
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Add steak, shrimp, onions and garlic to rice
  2. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce
  3. Mix everything together
  4. Add a small pinch of salt
