Alfredo Recipe

Description

I created this fettuccine Alfredo by modifying my mother's recipe. I get nothing but rave reviews when I make this dish. My girlfriend is a fettuccine Alfredo connoisseur, and she scrapes the pan every time. I must warn you, this recipe is not for the health-conscious!

This rich and creamy fettuccine Alfredo recipe is easy enough for beginner cooks, but it's impressive enough for company.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Seasonings (salt, pepper, garlic)
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese.
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce.
