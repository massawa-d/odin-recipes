Smash Burger Recipe

Description

Smash burgers are exactly what they sound like: burgers that have been smashed onto a hot griddle or skillet, sizzled until deeply browned, and cloaked with melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Beef patties
  • Cheese
  • Onions
  • Buns
  • Sauces (ketchup and mustard)
  • Seasonings (salt and pepper)

Steps

  1. Place beef patties on pan with onions and press till flat
  2. Add salt and pepper
  3. Place cheese onto one beef patty then place on top of the other
  4. Mix ketchup and mustard together and spread on buns
  5. Place beef patties in between the buns
